The following pre-conceptual schema only contains the nodes (and their relationships) required to represent the solution, in order to reduce its size and make it easier to read. Therefore, the syntax of the schema may not fit 100%.

The Game_designer, Requirement, Game_world, Design and Game classes were not needed in the solution, so they are not clickable. However, they remained to give the correct structure of the PCS.

Finally, the implications between dynamic relationships are not verified in this prototype because the solution did not want to force the user to follow a specific procedure when thinking about the game. However, these dynamic relationships still indicate the normal and recommended sequence to proceed in the prototype.
